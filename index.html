MediaManager by jeffWelling

Intelligently categorize your media.

MediaManager will intelligently attempt to identify your media based on it's filename. Intelligently, because it uses various permutations and matching methods to do better match things like roman numerals, numeric numbers, spelled-out numbers, various episode IDs, and titles and episode names. In the future, it will be expanded to encompass assisting you in sharing your media with your friends via sneaker-net, direct transfer, and any other p2p methods we can find.
